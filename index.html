Data Collection and Visualization

This semester began with small data and the data collection. We had to think of something pertaining to our digital lives that we could track with data. I decided to track my use of the Line app; I thought it might be unique since not many people outside of Asia use it. I tracked how often I used it, who I spoke to, and what language I spoke in amongst other factors. It was interesting having to be aware of what I was doing online. After collecting the data, I had to create a visual display of it. I used cartoon cactus plants to display my data. I thought it would be cute, fun, and easy to understand. Although Inkscape was challenging to work with at times, I'm glad to have learned what I did about data visualization.
